Overview

What is Zealous?

We are a team of law school graduates, designers, and engineers who are passionate about increasing everyone’s access to justice in the legal system. Our mission is to help you better navigate the legal system and connect with the best attorney possible for your particular circumstance. We do this by combining data analytics with intuitive design into one seamless experience.

What does Zealous mean?

To be "zealous" means to be passionate, driven, dedicated, and committed. It also holds special meaning within the legal profession, alluding to an ethical code that frames the unique responsibilities placed upon a lawyer;s shoulders — lawyers are bound by a duty to zealously advocate on behalf of their clients. At Zealous, we embrace and welcome that same responsibility upon ourselves.

Where does the name “Amino” come from?

Our mission at Amino is to connect everyone to the best health care possible and empower people to make confident decisions about their health care. Clarity starts with the basics, including which doctor you see, which treatment path you choose, and which hospital you visit. The name Amino alludes to amino acids, which are considered the building blocks of life, as we aim to help people assemble the building blocks of their care.

FINDING A LAWYER

Does this cost me money to use?

No, we do not charge the general public anything to use our service. Doing so would go directly against our mission statement to increase people’s access to justice in our legal system.

Why do we ask you these questions?

We crafted an adaptive questionnaire that probes and tests your self-reported facts given your chosen legal issue. The purpose behind this probing is to see what potential legal complications could be raised as red flags requiring further exploration when you meet with your attorney. This helps streamline the delivery of legal services while also providing you with a snapshot overview of your circumstances.

What happens after I submit a request?

Once you submit a request, your questionnaire is sent to attorneys who specialize in your particular legal issue and who are within your geographic area. And then, get on with your day. You’ll be hearing back from interested attorneys soon! Most importantly, you’ll have ultimate control of who you would like to meet — there is absolutely zero obligation to meet with any attorney when using Zealous.

Can anyone see my personal information?

No, they cannot. We understand that legal issues and/or complications can be a sensitive topic and confidentiality is one of our top priorities. Consistent with that, your contact information is kept hidden. Only when an attorney has expressed interest in meeting with you will your contact information be revealed to only that attorney.

Where is Zealous available?

We are currently conducting a beta trial of our platform solely in the state of Illinois. But fear not, we do have plans for expansion to other states.

Are you going to add other areas of law?

Yes, we are exploring other areas of law to add to our adaptive questionnaire. If there are any particular legal issues you would like us to complete faster, please let us know at founders@tryzealous.com.

Does Zealous provide legal advice?

We do not engage in providing legal advice — our aim is solely to provide generalized guidance to the public.
