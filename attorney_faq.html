Frequently asked questions

Are there any upfront costs?

None, it is completely free to sign up for Zealous and even receive lead recommendations. You will only be charged when you accept a lead AND the potential client actually receives your contact information.

How much does it cost?

The price per lead is currently $10, and you are only charged when you accept a lead. Our unique advertising structure allows you to stay on the platform as long as you want without paying any fees until you decide you are interested in a lead.

Will you share my information with anyone other than the leads I accept?

No.

How often will I receive emails?

You will only receive an email when we receive an inquiry from an actual person in your area that has a legal issue in an area of law that you practice.

How does Zealous work?

After a potential client fills out our comprehensive survey, we take the data taken from that survey and match it with several objective criteria, such as geographical fit and area of law, and match the client with attorneys in our network who meet the client’s criteria. If you match with the client you will receive an email with the basic information regarding the client’s survey answers to allow you to decide if you would like to accept the lead.

Is this a fee sharing agreement?

No, Zealous is only an advertising platform, because of this we charge a flat rate to match you with potential clients. This has no connection with any fees you may collect from any potential clients.
